Build the /vendor application for Divine Naturals that handles Head Vendor, Vendor, and Sub-Vendor hierarchy, fully integrated with Admin and Delivery Partner.
System Hierarchy (Vendor Module)
Head Vendor (Area Supervisor)
Example: Santa Cruz Head Vendor.
Can manage and monitor all vendors + sub-vendors under that area.
Has read/write authority for vendor operations.
Dashboard must include area-wide analytics.
Vendor (Main Vendor in an area)
Example: Santa Cruz Vendor.
Can handle own requirement, deliveries, earnings, and delivery partner assignments.
Can oversee their sub-vendors.
Sub-Vendor (Branch Vendor under Main Vendor)
Example: Santa Cruz → Vendor → Sub-Vendor (4 total).
Can manage local requirements, deliveries, and earnings.
Can log daily milk requirement, delivery, earnings, and assign drivers.

Requirement Tracking
Input fields: Today’s Requirement, Milk Circulated, Milk Delivered, Next Day Requirement.

Delivery Management

Track KYC of all delivery partners.
Alerts

Head Vendor Dashboard

Must-Have Features (All Vendor Roles)
Role-based login: Head Vendor, Vendor, Sub-Vendor.
Earnings breakdown: Filter by day/week/month.
Demand Forecasting: AI/logic to suggest requirements based on past data.
Notification System: Alerts for delivery, payment, KYC, requirement deadlines.
Search & Filter: Quickly find vendors, sub-vendors, delivery partners.
Export Reports: Excel/PDF for earnings and requirement reports.
